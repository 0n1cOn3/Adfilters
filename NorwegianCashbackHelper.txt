! This list is made so as to make it a lot easier on paper to use Norwegian cashback services without necessarily having to turn off your adblocker.
! This is because it'll prevent your adblocker from working on these sites, as adblockers have otherwise been known to interrupt cashback recognition mechanisms.
! It's currently tailored to users of Kickback, ViaTrumf and Norwegian Reward.
! Sites that focus on narrow-audience fashion or babies, sites that ship from China, very small sites, and sites that feature in the Norwegian adblock list (e.g. newssites, Viaplay), are NOT on this whitelist at the moment.
! Due to a near-complete lack of field testing, I hold NO RESPONSIBILITY if any cashbacks fail to be registered even if you're using this list.
